Important information on recent fake email activity
A number of UK banks have recently been targeted by fraudsters using emails to ask customers to enter their security details into a fake website.

At Alliance & Leicester we will never send you an email that asks you to verify your security details or link to Internet banking. If you receive an email claiming to be from Alliance & Leicester that you are suspicious about, please forward it to
suspiciousemails@alliance-leicester.co.uk

If you are worried that someone may already have your personal security details, then please contact us on 0844 800 11 44 (please dial +44 870 900 1199 if calling from outside the UK).
