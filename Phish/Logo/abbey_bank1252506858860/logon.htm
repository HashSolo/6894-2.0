Important warning about internet banking fraud
Customers of several UK banks have recently been the target of a fraud which uses fake e-mails to encourage customers to enter their card or security details into a counterfeit copy of their website. Abbey will never send you an e-mail asking you to enter, reconfirm or change your security details. If you think you may have revealed your security details in any way, or if you receive any suspicious looking emails please call us on: 0845 600 4388
